01 · The Story

Three pillars.
One product.

Every page, every deck, every asset returns to these. Privacy without compliance is evasion. Compliance without privacy is surveillance. shieldUSD is the third option.

Pillar 01
Private
by default.

Stealth UTXO commitments, shielded fees via DUST, and zero-knowledge proofs on every transfer. Privacy is the floor, not a feature.

Pillar 02
Auditable
by design.

Encrypted auditor memos are produced inside the proof itself. Compliance is honest by construction — users can't lie, regulators can't snoop.

Pillar 03
Built for
institutions.

Co-issued through licensed entities. MiCA-aligned in the EU, Genius/Clarity-aligned in the US. Designed with regulators, not around them.

08 · The Secret Layer

The brand
has secrets.

Every shieldUSD surface hides interactions you have to discover. Not gimmicks — the brand performing what it sells. People who notice them become evangelists. People who don't, get a clean institutional product. Both audiences served.

Secret 01
Burn Timer

A 60-second self-destruct counter pulses in the chrome. When it hits zero, the page redacts itself. Click to restore.

Secret 02
Whisper Mode

Type anywhere. Your characters appear at the bottom of the screen as an encrypted whisper, then disappear in five seconds.

Secret 03
Fingerprint Scrambler

A HUD element appears briefly: Canvas, WebGL, Fonts, Audio — all redacted. The brand reminds you it's not tracking.

Secret 04
Double-Agent Mode

Hold P for two seconds. The entire page inverts — black becomes white. Same brand, opposite world.

Secret 05
Steganography

The word MIDNIGHT is embedded in the grid pattern at 0.01 opacity. Visible only when you stare. Most never see it.

Secret 06
Mouse Trail Encryption

As you move, encryption noise (0 x ▓ ▒ Z K) trails behind the cursor and decays. Your motion, encrypted in real time.
